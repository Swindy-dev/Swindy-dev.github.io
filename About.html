About Us

Happy Tails Animal Adoption and Care Center is dedicated to finding loving homes for homeless pets. We provide a safe and nurturing environment for animals in need and strive to connect them with caring families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals while promoting responsible pet ownership and providing education and resources to the community.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate animal lovers, including veterinarians, adoption counselors, and volunteers, all working together to make a difference in the lives of pets and their families.
